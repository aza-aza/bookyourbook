RETURNS AND REFUNDS


Can I return a product?

RETURN WITHIN A WEEK
You can return a product with full cashback within a week of delivery if the order is above 6USD.
For other orders you get a refund of half the amount you paid. if the products are damaged we ensure a full payback.


Can i exchange a product?

As long as the product is not damaged we provide the choice of exchanging the product within 2 days of delivery.
